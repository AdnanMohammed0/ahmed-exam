## LCV 3 — Imaging in Complex Uropathy

**Total: 35 marks | BOARD | Time allowed: ~40 minutes**

**STAGE 1 — PRESENTATION**

**Q1a.** Which imaging investigations would you request next, and why? **[5 marks]**
